#  InsightForge AI: Business Decision Intelligence Agent

Transforms raw business data into actionable decisions through anomaly detection, root-cause reasoning, business recommendations, and what-if simulation powered by Fabric IQ concepts.

## Why InsightForge AI?

Business leaders often rely on dashboards that explain what happened, but not why it happened or what action should be taken next.

InsightForge AI acts as a Business Decision Intelligence Agent that:
- Detects anomalies in business performance
- Identifies likely root causes
- Recommends corrective actions
- Simulates future business scenarios

The result is faster, more informed decision-making.

Traditional dashboards tell businesses what happened.

They do not explain:

- Why it happened

InsightForge AI bridges this gap through multi-step business reasoning.

## Features

### 📊 Data Analysis

Upload business datasets in CSV format.

### 🚨 Anomaly Detection

Automatically identifies:

- Sales decline
- Inventory shortages
- Customer decline

### 🧠 Root Cause Analysis

Reasons about relationships between business metrics.

Example:

Sales decline → Inventory shortage → Revenue risk

### ✅ Recommendations

Generates business actions based on findings.

### 📋 Executive Summary

Creates a management-ready business summary.

### 🔮 What-If Simulator

Simulates business outcomes based on inventory changes.

### 🧠 Microsoft Fabric IQ Reasoning

Identifies business entities and reasons over relationships between:

- Sales
- Customers
- Inventory
- Regions
