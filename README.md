# 🧠 InsightForge AI

Business Decision Intelligence Agent

## Problem

Traditional dashboards tell businesses what happened.

They do not explain:

- Why it happened
- What actions should be taken
- What could happen next

InsightForge AI bridges this gap through multi-step business reasoning.

---

## Features

### 📊 Data Analysis

Upload business datasets in CSV format.

### 🚨 Anomaly Detection

Automatically identifies:

- Sales decline
- Inventory shortages
- Customer decline

### 🧠 Root Cause Analysis

Reasons about relationships between business metrics.

Example:

Sales decline → Inventory shortage → Revenue risk

### ✅ Recommendations

Generates business actions based on findings.

### 📋 Executive Summary

Creates a management-ready business summary.

### 🔮 What-If Simulator

Simulates business outcomes based on inventory changes.

### 🧠 Microsoft Fabric IQ Reasoning

Identifies business entities and reasons over relationships between:

- Sales
- Customers
- Inventory
- Regions

## Microsoft IQ Integration

InsightForge AI incorporates Fabric IQ concepts by identifying and reasoning over business entities such as Sales, Customers, Inventory, and Regions.

The agent performs multi-step reasoning to identify anomalies, explain causes, assess risk, and generate recommendations.
